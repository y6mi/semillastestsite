Discover Semillas Cantina

Located in Chino Hills, California, Semillas Cantina is a hidden gem in the world of Mexican cuisine and bars. This casual yet vibrant establishment offers a unique blend of delicious food and laid-back atmosphere, making it a perfect spot for groups to gather and enjoy a fun night out.


Semillas Cantina is not just your ordinary Mexican restaurant - it offers an array of enticing specials throughout the week, such as the popular taco & taquito Thursday where you can indulge in all-you-can-eat delights from their salsa bar. The friendly and attentive staff only adds to the overall experience, providing top-notch service that customers rave about.


The reviews speak for themselves, with patrons praising the fantastic service, great food, and welcoming ambiance. Whether you're stopping by for a casual meal or hosting a special event, Semillas Cantina goes above and beyond to ensure a memorable dining experience for everyone.


I had the pleasure of trying out their new brunch menu, and I must say, I was blown away. Each dish was bursting with authentic Mexican flavors, fresh ingredients, and impeccable presentation. From the mouthwatering tacos to the satisfying burritos, every bite was a true culinary delight.


If you're in the mood for a taste of Mexico in a lively setting, look no further than Semillas Cantina. With its diverse menu, welcoming atmosphere, and exceptional service, this hidden gem is definitely worth a visit.
